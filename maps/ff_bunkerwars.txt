    BUNKERWARS by R00Kie
__________________________________________________
Destroy the enemy bunker by attacking it as you would a player.
Defend your bunker from the enemy, get inside to deal extra damage.
The team that reaches 0 points first, loses.
__________________________________________________ 
Enter your bunker by climbing ontop of it.
	You Will Deal 2x more damage while inside and take 4x less damage.
Exit the bunker by jumping while in the center of it.
